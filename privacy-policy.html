Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 08, 2025

1. Who We Are

WeRun Consulting, including our mobile application EPM Slice, Powered by WeRun, is the data controller responsible for the personal data collected through our website, werunconsulting.com, and the EPM Slice app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you use our website contact form or EPM Slice app:

  • Username: To identify you when submitting inquiries or using the EPM Slice app.
  • Email Address: To reply to your inquiry or communicate with you via the EPM Slice app.
  • Message: To understand and address your query or request submitted via the website or app.

3. Why We Collect It

We collect this data solely to respond to inquiries or messages submitted via our website contact form or EPM Slice app. The data is processed through EmailJS (for website inquiries) or our app infrastructure to facilitate the delivery of your message to our team and to send you a response.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

To process your inquiry or app interactions, we use EmailJS, which receives the data you submit through our website contact form or EPM Slice app (username, email, and message). EmailJS processes this data to deliver your message to us and enable our response. For details on how EmailJS handles your data, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties, except as required by law.

5. Data Retention

WeRun Consulting does not retain your personal data beyond the time required to process and respond to your inquiry or message, whether submitted via the website or EPM Slice app. However, EmailJS may store your data as per their retention policies, which are outlined in their Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing their policy for more information.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit the processing of your data.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.

Since your data is processed by EmailJS, some rights (e.g., deletion or access) may need to be exercised directly with them, as outlined in their Privacy Policy. We will assist you to the best of our ability in coordinating with EmailJS to fulfill your requests.

7. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us at:

Email: info@werunconsulting.com

We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
