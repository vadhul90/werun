Survey Note: Detailed Analysis of Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS Updates for March 2024

This note covers the March 2024 updates (24.03) for Oracle’s Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS, based on documentation as of March 10, 2025, focusing on territory planning and audit enhancements.

Background and Context

March builds on earlier 2024 updates, introducing tools for sales alignment and compliance tracking.

Sales Planning Cloud Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • Dynamic Territory Mapping Tool:
    • New tool for real-time territory adjustments.
    • Impact: Simplifies sales territory management.
    • Implementation Consideration: Validate mappings with sales data.
    • Business Benefit: Faster territory realignment, e.g., adapting to market changes.

ePBCS Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • Enhanced Audit Logging:
    • Adds detailed metadata change logs.
    • Impact: Improves compliance tracking.
    • Implementation Consideration: Configure log retention policies.
    • Business Benefit: Easier audits with detailed change history.
