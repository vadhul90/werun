Survey Note: Detailed Analysis of Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS Updates for October 2024

This note examines the October 2024 updates (24.10) for Oracle’s Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS, based on documentation as of March 10, 2025, focusing on simulations and visuals.

Background and Context

October enhances analytical and presentation capabilities in Oracle EPM Cloud.

Sales Planning Cloud Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • Sales Scenario Simulation Tool:
    • New tool for running sales scenarios.
    • Impact: Improves what-if analysis.
    • Implementation Consideration: Test scenario accuracy.
    • Business Benefit: Better decision-making, e.g., testing price changes.

ePBCS Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • Dashboard 2.0 with 3D Charts:
    • Adds 3D chart options to Dashboard 2.0.
    • Impact: Enhances data visualization.
    • Implementation Consideration: Update dashboards with 3D charts.
    • Business Benefit: More engaging stakeholder presentations.
