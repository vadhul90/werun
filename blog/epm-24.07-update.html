Survey Note: Detailed Analysis of Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS Updates for July 2024

This note explores the July 2024 updates (24.07) for Oracle’s Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS, based on documentation as of March 10, 2025, focusing on notifications and infrastructure.

Background and Context

July enhances mobile engagement and completes a key infrastructure shift in Oracle EPM Cloud.

Sales Planning Cloud Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • Mobile Push Notifications:
    • Adds push notifications for quota alerts.
    • Impact: Improves real-time communication.
    • Implementation Consideration: Enable notifications in app settings.
    • Business Benefit: Instant alerts, e.g., notifying reps of quota changes.

ePBCS Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • OCI Migration for Classic:
    • Migrates all Classic environments to OCI.
    • Impact: Enhances reliability and scalability.
    • Implementation Consideration: Validate post-migration performance.
    • Business Benefit: More robust planning infrastructure.
