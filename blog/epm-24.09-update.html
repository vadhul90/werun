Survey Note: Detailed Analysis of Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS Updates for September 2024

This note covers the September 2024 updates (24.09) for Oracle’s Sales Planning Cloud and ePBCS, based on documentation as of March 10, 2025, emphasizing global planning and security.

Background and Context

September enhances multi-currency support and data security in Oracle EPM Cloud.

Sales Planning Cloud Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • Multi-Currency Support in Forecasts:
    • Adds multi-currency forecasting capabilities.
    • Impact: Simplifies global sales planning.
    • Implementation Consideration: Set up currency exchange rates.
    • Business Benefit: Unified global forecasts, e.g., consolidating regional data.

ePBCS Updates: Detailed Breakdown

  • Virus Scan on Uploads:
    • Adds virus scanning for uploaded files.
    • Impact: Enhances platform security.
    • Implementation Consideration: Monitor scan performance.
    • Business Benefit: Safer data imports.
