Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share eBooks in EPM Slice?

Open the “Insights Hub” section in the EPM Slice app. Locate the eBook you want to share and tap “Open in Books” to export the EPUB file to Apple Books. If you encounter an error on the first attempt, tap again—this typically resolves the issue automatically.

Why can’t I find EPM games?

Go to the “Games” section in the EPM Slice app. All games (Trivia, Scramble, Scenario Challenge, Process Builder Challenge, Crisis Command) are available offline, so no internet connection is needed. Ensure you have the latest app version installed from the App Store to access all game content.

How do I book a consultation?

Click the “Book a Free Consultation” button on the homepage or in the Consulting Services section. This will take you to the contact form below, where you can submit your details to schedule a free session with our EPM experts for EPBCS, Oracle BI, Essbase, or Sales Planning.

How do I access blogs?

Visit the “Insights Hub” section in the app to browse blogs on EPM updates, Groovy scripting tutorials, and best practices. To stay updated, enable push notifications in the app settings to receive alerts when new blog posts are published.

How do I participate in Community Q&A?

Log in or sign up for an EPM Slice account, then navigate to the “Community Q&A” section. You can post questions or answer others’ queries on topics like EPM General, Consolidation, Groovy Scripting, or Oracle BI. Engage with the community to share and gain knowledge.

What should I do if the app crashes?

First, try restarting the EPM Slice app. Ensure your device is running the latest iOS version. If the issue persists, note the feature you were using and any error messages, then contact us via the form below. Our support team will assist you promptly.
