Support for EPM Slice

How do I share eBooks in EPM Slice?

In the “Books” section, tap “Open in Books” to share EPUB files to Apple Books. If you see an error on the first attempt, try again—it resolves automatically.

Why can’t I find EPM games?

Ensure you’re in the “Games” section. Update to the latest app version via the App Store and check your internet connection for game content to load.

How do I request consulting services?

Visit the “Consulting Hub” in the app to request a free consultation for EPM implementation, optimization, or training. You can also contact us below.

Where are the blogs and industry news?

Access blogs and news in the “Insights” and “News” sections. Enable notifications in the app settings to stay updated on new content.

What should I do if the app crashes?

Restart the app and ensure you’re on the latest iOS version. If issues persist, contact us with details of the crash (e.g., feature used, error message).
