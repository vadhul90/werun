Suvai Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2025

Suvai, an offering of WeRun Consulting, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use Suvai (the "App"), a recipe app featuring AI-powered meal planning, ingredient dictionary, recipes, and more. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve Suvai’s services:

  • Account Information: When you create an account using an email address or Apple Sign-In, we collect your email address and username.
  • User-Generated Content: Recipes and meal plan preferences you create or upload are stored locally on your device.

We do not monitor or collect usage data, such as how you interact with the App or device information. We do not collect sensitive personal information (e.g., health data) unless voluntarily provided in meal plan preferences, and we do not use it for purposes beyond personalization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize Suvai’s features, such as generating AI-powered meal plans and storing recipes locally on your device.
  • Authenticate your account and ensure security.
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, or support issues (e.g., via email).
  • Comply with legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

WeRun Consulting does not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted providers (e.g., cloud storage for account data) to process data on our behalf, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations, such as court orders or regulatory requests.
  • Business Transfers: If WeRun Consulting is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the new entity, with continued privacy protections.

User-generated content, such as recipes and meal plans, is stored locally on your device and is not shared with us or third parties.

4. Data Security

WeRun Consulting implements industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your account data. User-generated content is stored locally on your device, and you are responsible for securing your device. You are also responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights under laws like the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) or GDPR:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correction: Request updates to inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at suvai@werunconsulting.com. We will respond within 30 days, or as required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information (email address and username) for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will remove your data within 60 days, except where required for legal or audit purposes. User-generated content stored locally on your device can be managed or deleted by you directly.

7. International Data Transfers

Suvai is operated from Australia, and your account data may be stored or processed in Australia or other countries where our service providers operate (e.g., cloud servers in the USA). We ensure appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, for international transfers to comply with GDPR and other privacy laws.

8. Children’s Privacy

Suvai is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately. Contact us at suvai@werunconsulting.com if you believe we have collected such data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

WeRun Consulting may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you via email or in-app notifications of significant changes. Continued use of Suvai after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Victoria, Australia, without regard to conflict of law principles, except where prohibited by local law, in which case the laws of your jurisdiction may apply. Any disputes arising from this Privacy Policy or your use of Suvai will be resolved in the courts located in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, unless local law requires otherwise.

11. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact WeRun Consulting at suvai@werunconsulting.com.
